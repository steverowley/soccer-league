The little men, recharted

The original dudes were Tiny-Terraces cartoons — skin tones, dyed hair, party hats, antennae. Charming, but off-brand. So everyone becomes a phosphor being: one Lunar-Dust body, a crisp Galactic-Abyss outline. Then two registers — Terraces keeps the chunk, the hair, the lively waving arms and a colourful kit shirt; Pictogram strips it to an austere upright with the kit rationed to a chest chevron. Flip between them in Tweaks; the Architect's Quantum-Purple eye marks the player in focus either way.
