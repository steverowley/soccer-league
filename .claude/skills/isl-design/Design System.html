II•Principles

Five laws of the league

The rules every ISL surface obeys. Break them only with intent.

01One voice, one faceSpace Mono carries everything — headline to data cell. Hierarchy comes from size and case, never from fading text.
02Ration the colour~90% of any screen is Lunar Dust on Galactic Abyss. Accents are reserved for meaning: live, focus, action, success.
03Square by defaultZero corner radius. Cards are hairline boxes — no fill, no shadow. Only avatars and status dots are round.
04Glow, don't dropElevation and energy are shown with glow, not drop shadows. Purple blooms signal the Architect; light glows signal hover.
05Imagery beside, never beneathHigh-contrast halftone space photography sits next to copy — text is never laid over an image.
